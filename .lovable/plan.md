
## New Features Plan

### 🌾 Farmer Tools
1. **Community Forum** — Farmers can post questions, share tips, and interact with each other
2. **Equipment Rental Marketplace** — List and rent farm equipment from other farmers
3. **Crop Price Ticker** — Real-time market prices for common crops on the dashboard

### 🛒 Consumer Experience
4. **Product Reviews & Ratings** — Rate and review purchased products
5. **Wishlist** — Save products for later
6. **Order Scheduling** — Choose preferred delivery date/time during checkout

### 🚚 Delivery & Logistics
7. **Multi-stop Route Optimization** — When a driver has multiple deliveries, show optimized route
8. **Earnings Analytics** — Weekly/monthly earnings charts with delivery history
9. **Delivery Ratings** — Consumers rate delivery partners after completion

### 📊 Admin & Analytics
10. **Revenue Dashboard** — Platform-wide revenue tracking with charts
11. **Automated Daily Reports** — Summary stats card showing daily platform activity
12. **User Activity Heatmap** — Show when users are most active

I'll implement the most impactful subset: **Crop Price Ticker, Product Reviews, Earnings Analytics, and Revenue Dashboard** — these add immediate value with backend integration.
